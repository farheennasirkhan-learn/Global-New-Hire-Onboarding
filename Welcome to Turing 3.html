As a new team member, your work supports Production Engineering’s ability to drive value for our clients.
Client
Client-first means our work must help the client succeed.
Clients bring us a need.
We do the work and deliver value back to them.
If something doesn’t clearly benefit the client, it’s unlikely to build a strong, long-term partnership.
Sales
Our clients partner with our Sales and technical teams to establish the key requirements needed for the scope of work (SOW). We are using 'SOW' as a placeholder term for any agreement with a client.
This is a deeply cross-functional process, and “Sales” is used as a placeholder to represent many different functions within Turing.
Together, this sets the foundation for all operational execution that follows.
Resourcing & Support
Once the Scope of Work is set, Fulfillment and Talent Acquisition act as the recruiting engines for Production Engineering, sourcing the world’s leading talent on behalf of Production Engineering.
Simultaneously, G&A functions (Finance, Legal, HR, Security), R&D, Marketing, and other teams support the process to ensure delivery execution remains innovative and runs smoothly.
Production Engineering
Production Engineering is where the magic happens. It’s the operational arm responsible for execution and creating value for our clients. We cannot achieve a “client-first” approach without all of us working together to empower Production Engineering.
